# Product Vision

## What It Is

WIAMarketingOS is an internal operating system for creating, launching, measuring and optimizing commercial campaigns.

It should turn a campaign idea into a full flow:

```text
campaign idea
landing page
form
lead
company
contact
opportunity
task
automation
conversion metric
learning for the next campaign
```

## What It Is Not

- A clinical CRM.
- A patient management system.
- One repository per campaign.
- A full HubSpot clone from day one.

## Product Principles

1. **Radical separation between product and acquisition**
   Client CRMs and internal marketing campaigns live in different systems.

2. **Campaigns as reusable assets**
   Dental, aesthetics or physiotherapy campaigns should be campaign records inside the same system, not separate repositories.

3. **Controlled generation, not visual chaos**
   AI should return validated structures rendered by safe blocks, not arbitrary HTML.

4. **Measurement from the beginning**
   Every landing page, form and CTA should create events. Without events, there is no learning loop.

5. **Compliance by design**
   Every lead should keep source, campaign, consent, date, IP or reasonable metadata and accepted policy.

6. **Sales assistance before blind automation**
   First prepare messages, prioritize leads and create tasks. Automate real sending only once the process is validated.

## Internal Users

- Management: sees performance, opportunities and pipeline.
- Marketing: creates campaigns, screens, variants and forms.
- Sales: qualifies leads, calls, logs activity and moves opportunities.
- Operations: reviews events, automations and data quality.

## North Star Metric

The primary metric should not be "number of landing pages created".

The primary metric should be:

```text
qualified opportunities generated per campaign and cost per opportunity
```

Secondary metrics:

- Visit-to-lead conversion.
- Lead-to-demo conversion.
- Demo-to-pilot conversion.
- Pilot-to-client conversion.
- Time to first contact.
- Estimated pipeline value per campaign.
